## Free Plan

**$0 forever**

The Free plan provides everything you need to start serving international customers.

### Features Included

- **Shopify Markets Integration** - Seamlessly works with your Shopify Markets configuration
- **Unlimited selectors for your store** - Create as many language, currency, and market switchers as needed
- **Unlimited page views** - No restrictions on traffic or usage
- **Personalized recommendations banner** - Show geolocation-based suggestions to visitors
- **24/7 basic support** - Email support available anytime

### Best For

- Stores just starting with international markets
- Basic language and currency switching needs
- Testing Selecty's features before upgrading

## Professional Plan

**$11.99 per month**
*or $107.91/year and save 25%*

Everything in Free, plus advanced selector types and automatic market redirects.

### Features Included

**All from Free, plus:**

- **Pro selectors & recommendations types** - Access to advanced selector layouts including Layered, Sidebar, Cascade, Scale, and Tape-line
- **Automatic markets redirects** - Automatically redirect visitors to their local market based on geolocation
- **24/7 chat, email, and phone support** - Priority support through all channels
- **7-day free trial** - Test all Professional features risk-free

### Best For

- Growing stores serving multiple markets
- Businesses wanting automatic customer routing
- Stores needing advanced selector designs

## Premium Plan

**$21.99 per month**
*or $197.91/year and save 25%*

Everything in Professional, plus full customization and multi-store management.

### Features Included

**All from Professional, plus:**

- **Titles and icons customization** - Fully customize text, labels, and icons in selectors
- **Connect multiple stores** - Link separate Shopify stores and route customers between them
- **Custom theme styles** - Advanced CSS customization for perfect brand matching
- **24/7 chat, email, and phone support** - Premium support with priority response
- **7-day free trial** - Experience all Premium features before committing

### Best For

- Established international businesses
- Stores with separate regional Shopify accounts
- Brands requiring perfect design alignment
- Businesses needing complete customization control

## Choosing the Right Plan

### Start with Free if you:
- Are new to international selling
- Need basic language and currency switching
- Want to test Selecty before investing
- Have a single market focus

### Upgrade to Professional if you:
- Serve multiple international markets
- Want automatic customer routing
- Need advanced selector designs
- Require priority support

### Choose Premium if you:
- Operate multiple regional stores
- Need complete design customization
- Want to connect separate Shopify accounts
- Require maximum flexibility

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    If you cancel your subscription, you'll retain access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will automatically switch to the Free plan. Your selectors and settings will be preserved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any hidden fees?">
    No. All pricing is transparent and displayed upfront. There are no setup fees, cancellation fees, or hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer 7-day free trials for Professional and Premium plans so you can test all features before being charged. For questions about refunds, please contact our support team at [support@devit.software](mailto:support@devit.software).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does annual billing work?">
    When you choose annual billing, you pay for 12 months upfront and save 25% compared to monthly billing. Annual subscriptions renew automatically after 12 months unless you cancel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
